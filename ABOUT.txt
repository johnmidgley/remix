ABOUT REMIX

Remix is a native macOS application for separating music tracks into individual stems (instruments and vocals) using state-of-the-art AI technology.

FEATURES
• 6-stem separation: drums, bass, guitar, piano, vocals, other
• Professional-quality AI model (Demucs v4)
• Native macOS interface with SwiftUI
• 8-band parametric EQ
• Variable speed and pitch control
• Real-time mixing and export

AI TECHNOLOGY

Remix uses Demucs v4 (Hybrid Transformer Demucs) developed by Meta AI Research for music source separation. Demucs achieves state-of-the-art quality with 9.0+ dB Signal-to-Distortion Ratio.
